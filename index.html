Bio

About Tamir Gluska

Tamir Gluska has always believed in forging his own path. Early in life, his love for adventure and self-reliance shaped a future driven by entrepreneurship and service. His journey into the wellness space began after searching for natural relief from a personal injury — leading him to discover the potential of CBD.

Determined to share this discovery, Tamir founded Green Planet in 2018, during a time when CBD was still widely misunderstood. Against industry challenges and without partners or investors, he opened his first store in Las Vegas, building the brand from the ground up.

When the COVID-19 pandemic hit, Tamir adapted quickly, launching an eCommerce platform that allowed Green Planet to reach customers nationwide. Through his leadership, the brand grew into a respected name, offering premium, USA-made CBD products and opening new retail locations in high-traffic destinations like Pier 39 in San Francisco and Waikiki, Hawaii.

    Tamir’s entrepreneurial drive didn’t stop there. Inspired to continue innovating in the wellness and lifestyle space, he expanded his portfolio with two new brands:
  • BloomStem CBD — A luxury CBD brand focused on sophisticated self-care and wellness rituals.
  • Si Scent — A fragrance and home goods brand that blends sensory pleasure with natural, eco-friendly elements.

Alongside his wife and business partner, Zheniya, Tamir has cultivated a company culture built on education, empowerment, and giving back. His businesses emphasize team training, community outreach, and exceptional customer service to create impactful, memorable experiences.

Today, as Green Planet and its sister companies continue to grow, Tamir remains deeply committed to innovation and service. His mission is clear: to make natural wellness solutions accessible, trusted, and transformative for as many people as possible.
